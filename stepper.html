This is a discussion of the use of stepper motors such as the 28BYJ-48 with Arduino microcontrollers. OSEPP has provided a < href="https://www.osepp.com/downloads/pdf/stepd-01-data-sheet.pdf" target="_blank"> wiring diagram of the 28BYJ-48 motor.

  1. OSEPP has a page discussing the use of the ULN2003A stepper motor board with the 28BYJ-48 stepper motors. In this case the orange wire is connected to ground and the pink, red, blue, and yellow wires are connected to the outputs of the ULN2003A chip.
  2. The stepper motor can also be used with an Arduino motor control shield such as the Wicked Device MOTO-4 or MOTO-6. The two coils in the motor are connected to the pink/red and blue/yellow pairs of wires. The wires for each coil are connected to a seperate motor output on the board.
